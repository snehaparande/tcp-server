PM Modi to lay AIIMS foundation, launch other projects in Uttarakhand's Haldwani today

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will on Thursday lay the foundation stone of the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) and launch several other projects worth over ₹17,500 crore in Haldwani in poll-bound Uttarakhand. This will be the second AIIMS in the hill state after the one in Rishikesh which was established back in 2012. Read more

Omicron fear, Section 144 imposed in Mumbai starting today till January 7

Mumbai Police has imposed Section 144 in Mumbai starting from today till 7th January 2022, in view of rising Covid cases, ANI reported on Thursday. As per the new COVID-19 restrictions, prohibition has been placed on New Year's celebrations, parties in any closed or open space including restaurants, hotels, bars, pubs, resorts and clubs from December 30 to January 7.
